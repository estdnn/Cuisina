Bibingka

Bibingka is a popular Filipino rice cake made with rice flour, coconut milk, and sugar. Traditionally baked in clay pots lined with banana leaves, it’s a soft and sweet treat often enjoyed during Christmas time. The dish is topped with salted eggs and cheese for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2-3 salted eggs, sliced
  • Grated cheese for topping
  • Banana leaves (optional, for lining the pan)

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). If using banana leaves, line the baking pan with the leaves.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the rice flour, coconut milk, sugar, water, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Beat in the eggs and melted butter until smooth.
  • Pour the mixture into the prepared pan. Place the sliced salted eggs and grated cheese on top.
  • Bake for 30-40 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  • Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade bibingka!

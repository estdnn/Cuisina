About CUISINA

CUISINA is a project developed by students of Sorsogon State University-Bulan Campus. Our aim is to preserve and celebrate the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines, allowing everyone to cook traditional Filipino dishes just like their Lolas (grandmothers) did. By providing easy-to-follow recipes and using locally sourced ingredients, we hope to bring the taste of home to every kitchen.

Our mission is to promote and preserve the culinary traditions of the Philippines by providing accessible and delicious recipes to home cooks around the world.

Our vision is to be the go-to resource for authentic Filipino recipes, inspiring home cooks to explore and appreciate the flavors of the Philippines.

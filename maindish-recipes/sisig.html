SIZZLING SISIG

Sisig is a classic Filipino dish that features a flavorful mix of chopped pork, onions, and chili peppers. Traditionally made from parts of a pig's head and liver, it's seasoned with calamansi, vinegar, and spices, then served sizzling. This beloved Kapampangan dish is a perfect mix of savory, tangy, and spicy flavors that makes it a favorite bar chow or meal accompaniment.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or pork face (maskara)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 red chili peppers, minced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Calamansi or lemon for serving

Steps

  • Boil pork until tender, then grill until the skin is crisp. Chop into small pieces.
  • Saut√© onions in butter, then add grilled pork and soy sauce. Mix well.
  • Stir in vinegar and chili peppers. Cook until the liquid is reduced.
  • Add mayonnaise, adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, and mix thoroughly.
  • Optional: Crack an egg on top and mix before serving.
  • Serve on a sizzling plate with calamansi on the side.
